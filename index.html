About

Building what matters, leading who matters

I've spent two decades in the trenches of engineering leadership — from early-stage startups to publicly traded companies. I've led teams at Block (Square), Slack, Quantcast, and Razer, shipping products used by millions.

As a Fractional CTO, I bring that depth to founders who need strategic technology leadership without the commitment of a full-time executive hire. Whether you're navigating your first technical hire or architecting for scale, I help you make decisions with confidence.

I'm especially passionate about helping companies leverage AI and modern tech stacks to create competitive advantages — turning technology from a cost center into a growth engine.
